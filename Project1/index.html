Welcome to Our Mapplication!

Our project aims to uplift students with mobile disabilities/impairments at UCLA by providing a space to share their overall feelings and stories regarding accessibility on campus. Our map is divided into three different regions on campus: the Hill, North Campus, and South Campus. Each region correlates to different survey responses, showcasing students' overall feelings toward campus accessibility—whether positive, negative, or neutral—and accounts of their experiences in that region that affected their overall sentiment towards campus accessibility. The regions are color-coded based on the students' overall feelings regarding accessibility: for example, if more students felt positively about accessibility based on their experiences in a certain area, it would be colored green.

Feel free to interact with our site by clicking on the regions of our map to look through the experiences students face in different areas on campus or share your own story.
