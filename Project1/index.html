Is UCLA Really Accessible?

Students with mobile disabilities are often overlooked and not considered by institutions when designing buildings or routes, which can greatly affect accessibility for these individuals. Here at UCLA, this is especially prominent because there are many hills, stairs, insufficient ramps, malfunctioning elevators, and inconvenient walkways that are not inclusive towards students with mobile impairments. Due to this, this platform provides students with mobile disabilities/impairments a place where they can share their experiences with accessibility on campus.
